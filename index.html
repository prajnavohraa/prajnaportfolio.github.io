About me

I am Prajna, a Computer Science and Design sophomore at IIIT Delhi. I am immensely passionate about working in the domain of Human Computer Interaction. I believe design is an essential element in the field of technology. It is more than just making something look good! Design plays a crucial role in how users interact with technology. From user interfaces to the physical design of a product, design is key to creating a seamless and intuitive user experience. I have always been excited to work on projects that fall in the intersection of design and tech. Specifically, I am deeply interested in the field of wearable technologies such as Virtual Reality and Augmented reality and in their applications to solve problems.
